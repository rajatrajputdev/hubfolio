History

Welcome to Juneja Agro Farms, where passion, tradition, and commitment come together to deliver the finest eggs to your table. Since our inception in 2006, our family-run business has been dedicated to providing high-quality, ethically produced eggs. With over two generations of experience in agriculture and farming, our journey has been a testament to hard work, innovation, and unwavering values.

What started as a humble dream has grown into a thriving enterprise that prioritizes the well-being of our animals, the environment, and, most importantly, our customers. At Juneja Agro Farms, we believe that great nutrition starts at the source. That’s why we follow ethical and organic farming practices to ensure that every egg we produce meets the highest standards of quality and care.

Our Philosophy

We are driven by the principle that good food is the foundation of a healthy life. Nutrition is not just about sustenance; it’s about nurturing the body and soul. This philosophy guides every step of our production process. From carefully selecting premium feed for our hens to implementing stringent quality checks, we leave no stone unturned in ensuring the best output.

Uncompromising Quality

We understand the importance of trust when it comes to what you put on your plate. That’s why we adhere to rigorous quality control measures at every stage of production. From collection to packaging, every egg undergoes thorough inspections to ensure it meets our high standards. Our hens are also fed with a balanced organic diet that is prepared inhouse to ensure that our hens lay eggs of superior quality. Our state-of-the-art facilities are designed to maintain hygiene and freshness, guaranteeing that only the best reaches your home.

Why us?

At Juneja Agro Farms, we pride ourselves on delivering eggs that are not just ordinary; they are an exceptional source of nutrition and flavor. Here’s why our eggs stand out from the rest:

Superior Nutritional Quality

Our eggs are packed with essential nutrients, making them an ideal choice for health-conscious individuals and families. Our eggs are a rich source of:
  • • Vitamin D3: Supporting bone health and boosting immunity.
  • • Protein: Each egg provides approximately 6-7 grams of high-quality protein, helping you meet your daily protein needs effectively.
  • • Vitamin A: Essential for vision, skin health, and overall immunity.
  • • Omega-3 Fatty Acids: Promoting heart and brain health.
  • • Antioxidants like Selenium: Aiding in reducing oxidative stress and improving overall cellular health.

Zero to Negligible Trans Fat

Unlike many other options available, our eggs are free from harmful trans fats. They are a wholesome, heart-healthy addition to your diet without compromising on flavor or quality.

Certified and Approved

Your trust matters to us. That’s why our eggs are certified and approved by leading authorities, including:

  • • FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority of India)
  • • FDA (Food and Drug Administration)
  • • ISO Standards Compliance

These certifications are a testament to our commitment to maintaining the highest levels of quality and safety.
